search for PID ACK for data packet

from keypress to PID ACK on USB line: 10 to 20ms

catting /dev/input/event0 in bash: 490us
printing via python3: 1ms

script below:

fff = open("/dev/input/event0", "rb" )
while 1:
  data = fff.read(24)
  print(data)
